Welcome to Ponte Vecchio Bistro and Bar, Killaloe

At Ponte Vecchio, we take pride in offering a delightful culinary experience that combines the best of traditional and contemporary cuisine. Nestled in the picturesque town of Killaloe, our bistro is a haven for food enthusiasts looking to indulge in a variety of delectable dishes, fine wines and rich coffee.

Savor the Flavours of Italy

Step into a world of authentic Italian flavors with our meticulously crafted menu. From classic sour-dough pizzas topped with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, delectable bruschetta to hearty mouth-watering soups serverd with fresh italian bread, every bite promises to transport you to the heart of Italy.

Drinks Intro Section

At the Ponte Vecchio Cafe, nestled in Killaloe, you can savor a delightful array of traditional Italian drinks. From the rich and robust flavors of espresso and cappuccino to the refreshing zest of Aperol Spritz and Limoncello, the cafe offers a true taste of Italy. Enjoy classic cocktails like Negroni or indulge in a selection of fine Italian wines, all while soaking in the historic ambiance of this charming location. Each drink at the Ponte Vecchio Cafe embodies the essence of Italian culture and craftsmanship, making it a perfect spot to experience authentic Italian beverages.

Secondhand Books and Deli at Ponte Vecchio Cafe

Ponte Vecchio Cafe isn't just a haven for lovers of Italian drinks, it's also a treasure trove for enthusiasts of secondhand books and gourmet delights. As you sip your espresso or Aperol Spritz, you can browse through a curated collection of secondhand books, each with its own unique history and charm. The shelves are filled with a variety of genres, offering something for every reader.

Delicatessen

In addition to literary finds, the cafe boasts a delightful deli section. Here, you can indulge in a selection of artisanal cheeses, cured meats, fresh bread, and other gourmet treats. Whether you're looking to enjoy a light snack with your coffee or gather supplies for a picturesque picnic by the Arno River, the deli offers a range of high-quality, locally sourced products. The combination of secondhand books and a fine deli creates a uniquely cozy and enriching experience at Ponte Vecchio Cafe.
